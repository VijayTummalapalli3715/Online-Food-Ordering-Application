About Bite Bazaar

A neighbourhood kitchen with a simple idea: honest food, made fresh, delivered fast.

From a food cart to your doorstep

Bite Bazaar started in 2019 as a single food cart serving burgers and cold brew outside a college campus. The queue kept growing, so we did too — first a small kitchen, then a full menu spanning pizzas, burritos, bowls, and desserts.

Going online was the natural next step. Manual phone orders meant mistakes, missed calls, and long waits. This app fixes that: you browse the full menu, build your cart, and check out in seconds — and our kitchen gets your order exactly as you placed it.

Everything is prepared to order. No heat lamps, no shortcuts.

Why order online with us

  • Real-time cart — see your total update as you build your order
  • Accurate orders — what you tap is what the kitchen sees
  • Contactless — order and pay from any device
